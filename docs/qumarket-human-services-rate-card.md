# QuMarket Add-ons and human-services rate card

Status: customer-facing draft calculated by QuCFA. It is not a binding quote and does not evidence real-time workforce availability. Every human engagement requires identity validation, capacity confirmation, an approved scope/SOW and final QuCFA authorization.

## Commercial model

| Work type | Billing model | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| Additional endpoints or users | Monthly per unit | Scales with recurring protected capacity |
| Bounded support request | Fixed per ticket | Predictable scope and closure criteria |
| Specialist, video or incident assistance | Per hour | Scope changes during diagnosis; incident response has a four-hour minimum |
| Connector, deployment, assessment or workshop | Fixed project/milestone | Requires defined deliverables and acceptance |
